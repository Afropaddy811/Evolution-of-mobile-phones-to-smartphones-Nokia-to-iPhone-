•	1980s–1990s: early mobile communication (Motorola, first Nokia phones).
•	2000s: Nokia’s rise — affordable, durable phones with global reach.
•	2007: iPhone launch — touchscreen, internet, App Store, new era.
•	2008–2015: Android’s rise, decline of Nokia, Samsung’s competition.
•	2015–Present: smartphone saturation, 5G, and AI integration.
