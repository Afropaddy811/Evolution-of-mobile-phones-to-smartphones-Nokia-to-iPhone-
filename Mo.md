# Competitive strategies: Apple vs Samsung vs Huawei. 


# Apples feels like the confident creative in the room. Employees are encouraged to express their ideas and challenge conventions, manifesting a culture that values Independence, equality, and innovation. Leadership is approachable, and hierarchy is flat, so people work collaboratively and can speak up freely. The company invests mostly in training programs that help staff grow and contribute creatively, making each person feel important to the company's success.



# Samsung Values adaptation and collective strength, doing hard work and discipline from Korean traditions with global business methods like Six Sigma. The environment supports teamwork, resilience, and life long learning. Career Development is struturd, and innovation is central to corporate life.

# Huwaie embodies a "wolf culture": Competitve, tenacious, and deeply team-Oriented. The company expects employees to quickly respond to market changes, presevere through challlenges, and support each other for shared success. Loyalty and collective responsibity are key, diving rapid growth and a wiligness to innovation at speed.

# apple is the inventive free spirit, Samsung the adaptable achiever, and Huawei the loyal team player.
